Welcome to UMOSO

This is the offical website for Umodzi Support Organisation (UMOSO) , started on 10th April,2019 by its Founder Mr. Veston Gondwe UMOSO is a non-profitable Organisation with an aim of assisting areas or people affected with Natural disasters such as Floods. UMODZI is a Chichewa word for Unity or Solidarity which is the state of being joined together or in agreement for a common Goal which in this case is to help those in need in times of a natural disaster strike.

VISION

Access to basic needs by every person even in times of a natural disaster ensuering that total loss of property does not lead to loss of life across all boaders.

Learn More

MISSION

To bring help in times of need to those that can not afford by offering free aid of basic needs with the aid of Volunteers and partners.

Learn More

GOAL

Saving lives of all people affected by natural disasters, ensuring safety of women and children and providing free aid for victims. VISION 2020
